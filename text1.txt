Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of computers, networking, storage, and other physical devices to store, process, and manage digital data. IT encompasses a broad range of activities, including the design, implementation, and management of hardware, software, and databases to support various organizational needs. It plays a key role in enabling communication, facilitating online transactions, supporting businesses with data analysis, and enhancing productivity in industries. In modern times, IT is integral to almost every aspect of life, from personal use of the internet to the management of large enterprise systems.
